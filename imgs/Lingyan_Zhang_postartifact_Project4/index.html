Susan lives in a small village beside a forest with her grandma. Life used to be peaceful, but one day the forest is enchanted by evil power. People say there are monsters in the forest. One day Susan's grandma gets into the forest and never comes back. Only more stories about monsters comes out. At the night with a full moon, Susan finally can't wait. She takes her small dagger with her and decides to find her grandma.
